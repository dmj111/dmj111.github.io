Here are some collected coffee notes that I often share with people.


I use an `Aerobie Aeropress`_.  A co-worker recommended it several
years ago, and it took me too long to finally try.  The hype on the
web page goes too far, but I am pretty happy with it.

.. _`Aerobie Aeropress`: http://aerobie.com/products/aeropress.htm

`This video`_ demonstrates the method I use, only I am less careful
and exact with how I do it.

.. _`This video`: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bzb6HcCVbYk


I use this `manual burr grinder`_, though the price has gone way up on
it since I bought one (good electric grinders cost a lot more than
this...).

.. _`manual burr grinder`: http://www.amazon.com/Kyocera-CM-50-CF-Ceramic-Grinder/dp/B003S9XF7K/


I buy green coffee beans from `Sweet Maria's`_.

.. _`Sweet Maria's`: http://www.sweetmarias.com/index.php


And roast it with a `heat gun`_.

.. _`heat gun`: http://www.homeroaster.com/heatgun.html
