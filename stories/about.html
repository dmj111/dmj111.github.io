About

I am a software engineer with a background in mathematics.

The programming languages I use the most are Python and C++, and I have written quite a bit of Matlab and F#. I like languages, though, and have dabled in OCaml, Racket, Common Lisp, Clojure, Javascript, Octave (ok... that comes with Matlab), and C.

Claiming to be a mathematician may be a bit too strong, but I will claim it anyway.

I have worked at MIT Lincoln Laboratory and Raytheon, spending some of my time there pretending to be a signal processing engineer.

My hobbies include home roasting coffee, and swimming, biking, and running. Slowly.
